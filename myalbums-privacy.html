Privacy Policy – My Albums

Last updated: June 23, 2026

1. Introduction

My Albums is an application that allows users to create, edit and export photo albums directly on their devices.

This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when using the application.

2. Information Collection

The application does not require:

  • User registration
  • Login
  • Name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Personal identification documents

The application does not directly collect personally identifiable information.

3. Local Storage

Albums, photos, layouts and application preferences are stored locally on the user's device.

This information is not transmitted to the developer.

Stored information may include:

  • Album titles
  • Photos selected by the user
  • Album layouts and pages
  • Application preferences
  • Premium purchase status

4. Advertisements

The application may display advertisements provided by third-party advertising services such as Google AdMob.

These services may collect limited device information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

Google AdMob Privacy Policy:
https://policies.google.com/privacy

5. In-App Purchases

The application offers optional in-app purchases through Google Play Billing.

Payment information is processed directly by Google and is never received, stored or processed by the developer.

6. Data Sharing

The developer does not sell, rent or share personal information with third parties.

7. Children's Privacy

The application is not directed specifically to children under 13 years of age.

8. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time.

Changes will be published on this page.
